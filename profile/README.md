## 🧬 &nbsp;Who We Are

**Softsasi** is a Bangladeshi technology company built by dreamers who believe technology should do more than power machines — it should power *people*.

We work across **software development**, **game creation**, **artificial intelligence**, **cybersecurity**, and **emerging technologies**. Every project begins with curiosity and ends with something that pushes the boundary a little farther than before.

> *"Softsasi is a playground for the bold, a lab for the restless, and a home for the strange and brilliant. We grow futures here. One idea at a time."*
